Campaign ROI Assumptions:
- Cost per outreach contact: $15
- Average annual plan value: $6,200 (NYC market)
- Conversion lift from targeted campaign: 10%
- These are simplified estimates — actual ROI depends on campaign execution, plan mix, and retention
 
Data Limitations:
- ACS 5-Year estimates have margins of error (not shown — MOEs available via Census API)
- PUMA boundaries don't perfectly align with neighborhood boundaries
- Small-area estimates (n=55 PUMAs) limit statistical power
- Bootstrap CIs on cluster rates reflect sampling variability, not measurement error
